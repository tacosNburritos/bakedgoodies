About Her

Who's behind BakedGoodiesByLeira's success?

She is Ms. Leira Franchesca Dumalay

She is not only the Founder and Head Baker of the business, but she also single-handedly manages every facet of its operations. From developing and executing marketing strategies to answering customer inquiries with personalized attention, she ensures every interaction reflects the business's commitment to quality. Her hands-on approach extends to the kitchen, where she meticulously bakes and designs each confection, ensuring they meet her high standards of taste and aesthetics. Beyond the creation of these treats, she also takes on the role of photographer and editor, capturing the beauty of her products for promotional materials. Finally, she oversees the packaging and shipping process, making sure each order is carefully prepared and dispatched to reach customers in perfect condition. Her dedication and multifaceted skill set are the backbone of the business, driving its success and growth.
