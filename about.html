History

BakedGoodiesByLeira began as a small home-based bakery in 2018, founded by Leira Martinez, a passionate baker with a vision for creating delightful and high-quality confections. Leira's love for baking started as a hobby, crafting treats for family and friends. As word of her delicious creations spread, she decided to turn her passion into a business. From its humble beginnings in Leira's kitchen, BakedGoodiesByLeira quickly grew, earning a reputation for its exceptional cakes and pastries. By 2020, the bakery had expanded, allowing Leira to open her first storefront, welcoming customers into a cozy space filled with the aroma of freshly baked goods.

Mission

At BakedGoodiesByLeira, our mission is to create exceptional, handcrafted cakes and pastries that bring joy and sweetness to every celebration. We are committed to using the highest quality ingredients, delivering impeccable customer service, and fostering a love for baking in our community. Our goal is to make every bite a delicious experience, adding a touch of sweetness to our customers' lives and making their special moments even more memorable.

Vision

Our vision is to be the leading cake shop known for innovation, quality, and a personal touch. We aspire to make every moment memorable with our delightful creations, becoming a cherished part of our customers' special occasions and everyday indulgences. BakedGoodiesByLeira aims to continuously evolve, offering new and exciting treats while maintaining our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.
